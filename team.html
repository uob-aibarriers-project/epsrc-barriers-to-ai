Postgraduate Researchers

Jana Alloush

Researcher

Jana Alloush is a PhD researcher in the School for Policy Studies at the University of Bristol. Her work focuses on forced migration, inequalities and interdisciplinary approaches to societal challenges.

Erin Brady

Researcher

Erin Brady is a Teaching Associate and Researcher in Engineering Education at the University of Bristol. Their work supports student inclusion and accessibility strategies for undergraduates in engineering.

Rosalba Castiglione

Researcher

Rosalba Castiglione is an ESRC-funded PhD researcher in the School for Policy Studies at the University of Bristol. Her research focuses on gender inequality and gender-based violence from an interdisciplinary perspective.

Weilin He

Researcher

Weilin He is a PhD researcher in Computer Science at the University of Bristol. Her research focuses on AI-assisted code generation, uncertainty estimation, and large language models.

Sven Hollowell

Researcher

Sven Hollowell is a PhD researcher in interactive artificial intelligence at the University of Bristol. His work explores the application of large language models in human–computer interaction.

Adrianna Jezierska

Researcher

Adrianna Jezierska is a PhD researcher at the University of Bristol Business School, studying how social media shapes the construction of legitimacy. Her work examines how online discourse and audience interactions influence which ideas gain acceptance over time.
